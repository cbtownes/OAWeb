Foundations & Endowments

Non-Profits’ have unique needs.

Obsidian Investment Advisers provides fiduciary investment management for not-for-profit organizations, trusts, and endowments. As such,we are sensitive to their unique needs like providing models showing how far into the future funds may last, and being able to work comfortably with tax professionals to meet the demands of Form 990 or 1120-H filings. Sometimes, an early-stage organization has not yet established or adopted a clear investment policy for its funds. In these instances we work closely with the organization's Board of Directors and/or Investment Committee to craft an investment policy statementthat meets the organization's needs.

Community Associations

Is your association positioned for higher returns?

When board members are novice investors and manage an association’s reserve funds, the result is often sub-par returns. Obsidian Investment Advisers understands the needs of community associations, and has over 10 years’ experience in successfully guiding them in establishing investment policies and decisions. We utilize a complex proprietary analysis, which takes into consideration the cash flow dynamics of current investments in the portfolio and timing and amount of future reserve expenses.
